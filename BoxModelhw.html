Pizza

Pizza is a savory dish of Italian orgin consisting of usually round, flattened base of leavened wheat-based dough topped with tomatoes, cheese, and often various other ingredients(such as anchovies, mushrooms, onions, olives, pineapple, meat, etc.), which is then baked at a high temperature, traditionally in a wood-fired oven. A small pizza is sommetimes called a pizzetta. A person who makes pizza is known as a pizzaiolo.
