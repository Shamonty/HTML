Arabian Chicken Shawarma

Tender, marinated chicken slow-cooked with exotic spices, wrapped in warm bread with fresh vegetables for a flavorful, satisfying bite every time.

Ingredients

For the chicken:

  • 750g 7 large boneless skinless chicken thighs
  • 1 onion, thinly sliced
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • ½ lemon, juiced
  • 4 tablespoons olive oil, divided

For the seasoning mix:

  • 2 teaspoons cumin powder
  • 2 teaspoons paprika
  • 1 teaspoon allspice
  • 1 teaspoon cinnamon, ground
  • ½ teaspoon clove, ground
  • ½ teaspoon cardamon, ground
  • ¼ teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • 1 teaspoon salt

Instructions

  1. 1
    Pre-heat oven

    Pre-heat oven to 450ºF.

  2. 2
    Cut the chicken

    Cut chicken thighs into strips, roughly ½ inch thick.

  3. 3
    Make a marinade

    Mix the lemon juice and 1 tablespoon of the olive oil with all the spices and combine to create a paste.

  4. 4
    Marinate the chicken

    Mix the marinade with the chicken strips and the sliced onions. Marinate the chicken for a few hours in the fridge, or ideally overnight.

  5. 5
    Cook the chicken

    Place 3 tablespoons of olive oil on a large sheet pan and evenly spread it out. Heat the empty sheet pan for 5-7 minutes in the oven. Lay chicken on the heated baking sheet and bake at 450ºF for 15-20 minutes until fully cooked through. Test one piece to ensure its white on the inside and that juices run clear.

  6. 6
    Wrap the chicken with your preferred toppings in the flatbread and serve.

    You can broil for a few minutes if you want additional color on the chicken. Serve the Shawarma with toum sauce or yogurt tahini sauce, pita bread and an assortment of your favorite vegetables to make a wrap.
