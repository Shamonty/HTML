Spicy Penne Pasta

A vibrant and flavorful Italian pasta dish with a kick of spice. Tossed with fresh tomatoes, garlic, and chili peppers for an unforgettable meal.

Ingredients

  • 16 oz penne pasta
  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced or grated
  • ½ teaspoon crushed red pepper flakes
  • 2 teaspoons Italian seasoning
  • 28 oz can crushed Italian tomatoes
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • ½ teaspoon salt & pepper

Instructions

  1. 1
    Make the Sauce

    Heat the oil over medium-high heat in a large skillet. Sauté the garlic for 30 seconds, then add the red pepper flakes and Italian seasoning and toast the spices for another 30 seconds.

  2. 2
    Simmer the Sauce

    Add the crushed Italian tomatoes, sugar, salt, and pepper, and stir to combine. Bring the sauce to a low simmering boil, then reduce the heat to low, cover, and let it simmer for 20 minutes.

  3. 3
    Cook the Pasta

    Cook the pasta according to the package directions. Before draining, reserve 1 cup of the pasta water and set it aside. Drain the pasta.

  4. 4
    Combine

    Combine the pasta with the sauce and ½ cup of the reserved pasta water. Toss to combine well, turn off the heat.

  5. 5
    Serve

    Serve topped with parmesan cheese.
