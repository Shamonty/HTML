Aromatic Chicken Biryani

Fragrant basmati rice layered with tender, spice-marinated chicken, slow-cooked with aromatic herbs and rich flavors. Every bite of chicken biryani delivers a warm blend of spices, juicy meat, and perfectly fluffy rice that turns a simple meal into a feast.

Ingredients

Marinade for chicken biryani:

  • ½ kg chicken, skinless bone-in or boneless, large pieces such as thighs, drumsticks, legs or mix of all
  • 3 tablespoons plain yogurt
  • 1¼ tablespoons ginger garlic paste, 1 inch garlic and 4 garlic cloves crushed
  • ½ to ¾ teaspoon salt, adjust to taste
  • ¼ teaspoon turmeric
  • ½ to 1 teaspoon red chilli powder or paprika
  • ½ to 1 tablespoon garam masala or biryani masala
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice, optional

Whole spices:

  • 1 bay leaf
  • 4 green cardamoms
  • 6 cloves
  • 1 inch cinnamon
  • 1 star anise
  • ¾ teaspoon shahi jeera or caraway seeds, substitute with cumin
  • 1 strand mace

Other ingredients for chicken biryani:

  • 2 cups basmati rice, aged rice only
  • 2 tablespoon ghee or oil
  • 1 large onion, sliced thinly
  • ¼ to ½ cup mint leaves, chopped fine, about 15 leaves
  • 1 green chilli, slit or chopped
  • ¼ cup plain yogurt
  • ¼ to ½ teaspoon red chilli powder
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala or biryani masala
  • 3 cups water
  • ¾ teaspoon salt
  • 2 tablespoon fried onions
  • 1 pinch saffron, optional, soaked in 2 tbsps hot milk

Instructions

  1. 1
    Preparation

    To a bowl, add yogurt, ginger, garlic paste, salt, garam masala, tumeric, lemon juice and red chilli powder. Mix up well and taste the marinade. Add more salt and spice if needed. Make slits on the chicken pieces. Add it to the marinade and mix well. Cover and set aside for 1 hour to overnight. Rinse basmati rice thrice and soak for 30 minutes. Drain to a colander after 30 minutes.

  2. 2
    Add ghee or oil in a pot and add spices

    Heat ghee or oil in a pot or pressure cooker. Add whole spices- bay leaf, cardamoms, cloves, cinnamon, star anise, shahi jeera and mace. Skip them if you do not have.

  3. 3
    Add onions, chicken and cook

    When the spices begin to sizzle, add onions and fry them evenly stirring often until uniformly light brown but not burnt. Add chicken and saute until pale for 5 mins on a medium heat. Cover and cook on a low flame until the chicken is soft cooked or tender. Saute to evaporate any excess moisture in the pot.

  4. 4
    Add yogurt, spices and others

    Taste test and add more salt if needed. Add yogurt, half of the chopped mint leaves, slit green chilli, red chilli powder and garam masala. ½ to 1 teaspoon red chilli powder, 1 green chilli, ¼ cup plain yogurt, 1 teaspoon garam masala.

  5. 5
    Layering

    Mix everything well and layer chicken evenly at the bottom. Spread drained rice in a layer over the chicken. To a separate pot, pour water. If cooking in a pot use more water

  6. 6
    Cooking the Chicken Biryani

    Add salt and mix. Taste the water. It must be slightly salty. Bring this to a rolling boil. Pour 2 cups of this hot water across the sides of the cooker or pot. Gently pour the rest on top of the rice. Level the rice if needed. Sprinkle the rest of mint leaves. Optionally add fried onions and saffron soaked milk if using.

  7. 7
    Pot method

    If cooking in a pot, cover and cook on a medium flame until the rice in the chicken biryani is cooked completely but not mushy or undercooked. If the rice is still under cooked or hard and there is no water at the bottom, pour ¼ to ½ cup more boiling water depending on the kind of rice used and cook further. When done it has to be fully cooked, yet remain grainy but not mushy.

  8. 7
    Cooker method

    Set the flame to medium high.Cover and place the whistle.Cook until you hear 1 whistle. Move the cooker away from the burner to stop cooking further.

  9. 8
    Serve

    Rest until the pressure drops naturally. Fluff up the chicken biryani with a fork. Serve from top to the bottom layer. Each serving gets biryani rice from top and chicken from the bottom layer. Enjoy chicken biryani with raita and sliced vegetables.
