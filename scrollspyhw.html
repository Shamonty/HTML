What are hobbies?

Hobbies are activities people enjoy doing in their free time, not because they have to, but because they want to. They help us relax, reduce stress, and make us feel more happy and interested. Hobbies may include things like reading books, drawing, and gardening to playing sports, collecting stamps, or learning music. They allow people to express their creativity, develop new skills, and sometimes even connect with others who share the same interests.
